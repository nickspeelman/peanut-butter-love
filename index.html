Peanut Butter Love

Celebrating our 6th anniversary by filling backpacks with peanut butter

We’re collecting 16 oz jars of peanut butter for our church’s Backpack Ministry , which discreetly sends home weekend meals with students facing food insecurity. They currently pack about 100 bags every week.

Our goal: 2,192 oz — one oz for every day of marriage. That’s 137 lb or 137 jars (16 oz each).

Every donation enters you into a raffle to win a peanut-butter-themed gift basket. You’ll receive 1 raffle entry per 16 oz jar or per $2 donated. The drawing will take place on October 12.
